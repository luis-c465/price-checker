Product Details

About This Product

Husky has taken the ratcheting wrench design and applied it to the tap and die application. The ratcheting handles and adapters allow for easy back-and-forth movement that's required while cutting threads on either taps or dies. The 5-degree ratcheting arc with reversing lever eliminates hand-over-hand turning of the T-handle wrench. The hex die adapters work on either round or hex-shaped dies.

Highlights

  • Set includes a 72-tooth ratcheting T-handle, a sliding T-adapter, 34 hex dies, 34 taps, 2 pitch gauges (SAE & Metric), small tap adapter (0.11-0.191 in.), medium tap adapter (0.219-0.280 in.), 2 die adapters and a 4 mm hex key
  • Hex Dies include SAE sizes 4x40, 6x32, 10x32, 8x32, 10x24, 12x24, 1/8x27, 1/8x28, 1/4x20, 1/4x28, 5/16x18, 5/16x24, 3/8x16, 3/8x24, 7/16x14, 7/16x20, 1/2x13 and 1/2x20. Also includes Metric sizes M3x.05, M3x0.6, M4x0.7, M4x0.75, M5x0.8, M5x0.9, M6x1, M7x1, M8x1.25, M9x1, M9x1.25, M10x1.25, M10x1.5, M11x1.5, M12x1.5 and M12x1.75.
  • Taps include SAE sizes 4x40, 6x32, 10x32, 8x32, 10x24, 12x24, 1/8x27, 1/8x28, 1/4x20, 1/4x28, 5/16x18, 5/16x24, 3/8x16, 3/8x24, 7/16x14, 7/16x20, 1/2x13 and 1/2x20. Also includes Metric sizes M3x.05, M3x0.6, M4x0.7, M4x0.75, M5x0.8, M5x0.9, M6x1, M7x1, M8x1.25, M9x1, M9x1.25, M10x1.25, M10x1.5, M11x1.5, M12x1.5 and M12x1.75.
  • Set Includes both British Standard Pipe (BSP) and National Pipe Thread (NPT) Taps and Dies
  • Set Includes both National Coarse (NC) and National Fine (NF) Taps and Dies
  • Set Includes both Taper and Plug style Taps
  • Ratcheting T-handles allow a 5-degree swing arc and have a reversing lever, eliminating hand-over-hand turning
  • Twist lock guide system reduces walk back of the die guide and stays centered while cutting
  • Die adapters work with the 3/8 in. drive pass-thru ratchet and socket system as well as socket accessories
  • Spring-loaded cap allows for quick-release of large tap adapter
  • Blow mold carrying case with tilted tap holders for easy access and secure storage
