About This Product

GEARWRENCH ratcheting tap and die T-handle systems are perfect for the master mechanic or machine Shop. This 114-piece set comes with blow molded case for safe and organized storage. A 5 ratcheting arc with reversible lever eliminates hand-over-hand turning. Twist Lock guide system reduces back walk of die guide extension adapters, providing access in tight confined areas. The length of tap and die system can be extended by combining adapters and any 3/8 in. drive extension. Tap holders tilt down for easy access, keeping taps secure and organized. The die adapter works with round and hex-shaped dies.

Highlights

  • Die adapters work on round and hex-shaped dies
  • Ratcheting T-handles wind dies with a 5 swing arc and have a reversing lever to eliminate hand-over-hand turning
  • Twist Lock guide system reduces the "walk back" of the die guide and keeps the dies centered while cutting
  • Ratcheting T-wrench and die adapters work with the 3/8" drive Pass-Thru ratchet and socket system as well as socket accessories
  • Auto-locking feature allow the tap adapters to be removed safely
  • Drive extension adapters provide greater access in confined areas when used with 3/8" drive extensions
  • Blow mold carrying case with tilted tap holders for easy access and storage
  • Set includes 2 ratcheting T-handles, 3 sizes of tap adapters and 2 sizes of die adapter, 2 extension adapters and a sliding T for the adapters, a 4mm hex key, 5 screw extractors, SAE and Metric thread gauges, and 48 taps and dies in SAE and Metric sizes ranging from 4 x 40 NC to 3/4" and 3mm to 18mm
